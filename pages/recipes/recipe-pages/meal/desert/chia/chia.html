Chia Pudding

prep time: 5 MINS | total time: 2 HRS 5 MINS | yield: 10 SERVINGS

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#DESERT #SWEET #SUMMER

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • ¼ cup chia seeds
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 tbsp honey, maple syrup, or agave
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Raspberries for serving

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a bowl, mix milk, chia seeds, honey, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Cover and refrigerate until thickened, for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. Add raspberries or preferred mix-ins and toppings before serving.
Berry Bliss

Before serving, layer the chia pudding with a mixture of fresh berries like strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries. Top with a sprinkle of granola or chopped nuts for added texture.

Cocoa Crunch

Mix in a spoonful of cocoa powder to the chia pudding mixture before refrigerating. Once thickened, layer it with banana slices and a drizzle of nut butter. Top with a scattering of chocolate chips or cocoa nibs.

Tropical Delight

Incorporate some diced mango, pineapple, and toasted coconut flakes into the pudding before refrigerating. When ready to enjoy, garnish with a touch of mint leaves for a refreshing tropical twist.

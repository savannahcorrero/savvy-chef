Smoothie Bowls

total time: 10 MINS | yield: 1 BOWL

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#DESERT #SWEET #SUMMER

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 frozen ripe banana, sliced
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • ½ cup frozen berries (strawberries, raspberries, blackberries, blueberries)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds
  • 2 tbsp granola
  • 1 tbsp coconut flakes
  • 1 tsp pink pitaya powder

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a food processor or powerful blender, place the frozen banana slices and milk, blend until the mixture becomes creamy in texture.
  2. Add ½ cup of frozen berries and blend until fully combined.
  3. Transfer the smoothie into a bowl and finish by adding chia seeds, granola, coconut flakes, and pitaya powder.
Nutty Bliss Bowl

Add a tablespoon of almond butter or peanut butter to the smoothie mixture for extra creaminess and nutty flavor. Top with chopped nuts (like almonds, walnuts, or pistachios) for crunch and added nutrition.

Tropical Paradise Bowl

Include a handful of frozen tropical fruits like mango, pineapple, or papaya along with the mixed berries for a vibrant and refreshing twist. Garnish with shredded coconut, a drizzle of honey, and a few fresh mint leaves for a tropical flair.

Chocolate Berry Delight

Mix in a tablespoon of cocoa powder to the smoothie for a delicious chocolate-berry combination. Top with dark chocolate chips or cacao nibs, crushed pretzels, and a dollop of Greek yogurt for a delightful blend of sweet and salty flavors.

Muddy Walnut Cake

prep time: 30 MINS | cook time: 37 MINS | additional time: 3 HRS 5 MINS

total time: 4 HRS 10 MINS | yield: 12 SLICES

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#DESERT #SWEET #FALL

INGREDIENTS

CAKE:
  • 2 cups walnuts
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
FROSTING:
  • 3 cups chopped chocolate chips
  • ¾ cup milk of choice
  • ¾ cup coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ tsp salt

INSTRUCTIONS

CAKE:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Scatter 3 cups of walnuts on a baking sheet. Bake for 7-12 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes, until they exude a rich aroma. Set aside to cool for 5 minutes as you prep the batter.
  2. Maintain the oven at 350°F and line a circular pan with parchment paper. In a large bowl, mix sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Coarsely chop 1 cup of the cooled, toasted walnuts and set aside. Mix eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla into the dry mixture. Mix for 2 minutes, then pour in boiling water. Gently fold in the chopped walnuts.
  4. Pour the batter into the parchment-lined pan. Bake for 22-48 minutes until a toothpick comes out with tender crumbs. Allow to cool completely (2-3 hours) before moving on to frosting.
FROSTING:
  1. Over gentle heat, melt together chocolate, milk, coconut oil, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt in a small saucepan. Stir till velvety and blended.
  2. Allow it to cool for 15-20 minutes (until it slightly thickens).
  3. Coarsely chop the remaining walnuts, drizzle the frosting over the cooled cake and sprinkle with chopped walnuts.
Fruit Infusion

Add a layer of your favorite fresh berries or sliced fruits between the cake layers for added color and flavor. Consider using raspberries, strawberries, or even sliced peaches.

Mocha Twist

Infuse a coffee flavor by adding 1-2 tablespoons of instant coffee granules to the boiling water in the cake batter. For the frosting, blend in a touch of coffee extract or strong brewed coffee to the chocolate mixture.

Creamy Filling

Create a luscious filling by spreading a layer of whipped cream, cream cheese, or a flavored mousse between the cake layers.

Raspberry Muffins

prep time: 10 MINS | cook time: 20 MINS

total time: 30 MINS | yield: 6 MUFFINS

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#DESERT #SWEET #SUMMER

INGREDIENTS

  • 1¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ¾ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp kosher salt
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 6 oz raspberries
  • 2 tbsp sparkling sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Place paper liners in a muffin tin and lightly spray with vegetable spray.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk the egg. Add buttermilk, vegetable oil, and almond (or vanilla) extract. Whisk to combine.
  4. Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture and stir until just combined. Fold in the raspberries gently.
  5. Divide the batter evenly into the prepared muffin cups. Bake for 18-22 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
Streusel Topping

Create a simple streusel topping by mixing together brown sugar, flour, and melted butter. Sprinkle this on top of the muffin batter before baking to add a delightful crunchy texture and extra sweetness.

Cream Cheese Swirl

Mix softened cream cheese with a bit of sugar and vanilla extract. After filling each muffin cup with batter, drop a spoonful of the cream cheese mixture on top and swirl it gently with a toothpick for a tangy and creamy surprise inside.

Citrus Zest

Add a burst of flavor by incorporating citrus zest into the batter, such as lemon or orange zest. This will give the muffins a refreshing and aromatic twist that complements the raspberries beautifully.

Coconut Snowballs

prep time: 15 MINS | additional time: 24 HRS

total time: 24 HRS 15 MINS | yield: 24 SLICES

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#DESERT #SWEET #WINTER

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 box chocolate cookie wafers
  • 2 cups pecans
  • ¾ cup powdered sugar
  • ¼ cup high-quality cocoa
  • 3 tbsp light corn syrup
  • 1 cup flaked coconut, for rolling

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a food processor, finely process the flaked coconut and set it aside in a shallow dish.
  2. Process the wafers and pecans in the food processor until they become large crumbs.
  3. Transfer the wafer and pecan mixture to a large bowl and stir in powdered sugar and cocoa.
  4. Add the corn syrup and mix thoroughly (you might need to use your hands).
  5. Roll the mixture into small balls using your hands or a cookie scoop.
  6. Roll each ball in the reserved coconut.
  7. Place the chocolate coconut balls in an airtight container and let them sit for at least a day to develop flavors.
Fruit Twist

Add a burst of fruity flavor by incorporating finely chopped dried fruits like apricots, cherries, or raisins to the mixture before rolling into balls. The natural sweetness and varied textures will add a delightful twist.

Gourmet Coating

Experiment with different coatings for the truffles. Instead of only using flaked coconut, roll some of the truffles in crushed nuts, cocoa powder, or even colorful sprinkles to create a visually appealing assortment.

Chocolaty Dip

Melt high-quality chocolate (dark, milk, or white) and dip the chilled truffles into the melted chocolate. Allow them to set on parchment paper before serving. The chocolate coating will add an extra layer of richness and indulgence.

Sesame Chicken

prep time: 15 MINS | cook time: 15 MINS

total time: 30 MINS | yield: 4 SERVINGS

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#DINNER #SAVORY #SUMMER

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts , chopped
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 3 tbsp corn starch
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, and sesame oil to make the sauce. Set aside.
  2. In three separate bowls, place beaten egg, 3 tablespoons of corn starch, and coat the chicken pieces by dipping them in the egg, then corn starch.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Cook the chicken pieces until fully cooked and golden brown. Transfer the cooked chicken to a plate.
  4. In the same pan, add a little oil if needed, and sauté the fresh spinach leaves until wilted, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the chicken back to the pan with the sautéed spinach. Pour the sauce over the chicken and spinach, tossing everything together until well coated.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds if desired. Serve the honey sesame chicken with spinach over cooked rice.
Garlic + Ginger

To add an extra layer of flavor, infuse the sauce with minced garlic and grated ginger. Before whisking the honey, soy sauce, and sesame oil together, sauté minced garlic and grated ginger in a little oil until fragrant. Then, combine with the sauce. The garlic and ginger will elevate the sauce, giving it a kick and making the dish more aromatic and exciting.

Nutty Crunch

Sprinkle some toasted sesame seeds and chopped roasted peanuts over the finished dish before serving. The toasted sesame seeds will complement the sesame oil in the sauce, and the chopped peanuts will add a satisfying crunch to each bite, elevating the texture and taste of the dish.

Caprese Salad

total time: 10 MINS | yield: 6 SERVINGS

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#LUNCH #SAVORY #SUMMER

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 lb fresh mozzarella
  • 1 bunch fresh basil
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella into ¼-inch thick slices.
  2. Layer slices of tomatoes and mozzarella on a large, shallow platter. Arrange the basil leaves on top of the mozzarella.
  3. Drizzle the salad with extra-virgin olive oil and season with salt and pepper according to your taste.
Balsamic Glaze

Drizzle a balsamic glaze over the tomato and mozzarella salad to add a sweet and tangy flavor. You can either use store-bought balsamic glaze or make your own by reducing balsamic vinegar until it becomes thick and syrupy.

Fresh Herbs

Sprinkle some freshly chopped herbs like basil, parsley, or oregano over the salad. The aromatic herbs will enhance the flavors and add a burst of freshness to the dish.

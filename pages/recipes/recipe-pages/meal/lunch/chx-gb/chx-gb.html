Sesame Chicken +
Green Beans

prep time: 5 MINS | cook time: 25 MINS

total time: 30 MINS | yield: 6 SERVINGS

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#LUNCH #SAVORY

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 lb green beans
  • ½ cup coconut aminos
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp arrowroot powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame seed oil
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds
  • Salt and pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a bowl, combine coconut aminos, chicken broth, arrowroot powder, honey, sesame seed oil, rice vinegar, and grated ginger. Whisk to make the sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken thighs, season with salt and pepper, and cook until golden brown, stirring occasionally. Transfer the cooked chicken to a large mixing bowl.
  3. In the same skillet, cook the green beans until soft, stirring occasionally. Transfer the green beans to the same mixing bowl.
  4. Return the cooked chicken and green beans to the skillet. Pour 1 cup of the sauce over them and cook for another 3 minutes to heat through and thicken the sauce. Toss the ingredients to coat them in the sauce.
Colorful Bell Peppers

Include a mix of sliced red, yellow, and green bell peppers to the stir fry. The vibrant colors not only make the dish visually appealing but also add a variety of flavors and nutrients.

Toasted Nuts

For some added crunch and richness, sprinkle toasted cashews or almonds over the finished stir fry. The nuts will provide a delightful texture contrast and elevate the overall taste of the dish.

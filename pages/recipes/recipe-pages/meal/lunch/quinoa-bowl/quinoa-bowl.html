Quinoa Bowls

total time: 40 MIN | yield: 10 SERVINGS

DIFFICULTY

TAGS:
#LUNCH #SAVORY #SWEET #SUMMER

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup uncooked quinoa, rinsed
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • 11 ounce can mandarin oranges
  • ¼ cup red onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme leaves
  • ½ cup balsamic vinaigrette

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook the quinoa according to the package directions, using chicken stock instead of water for extra flavor.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, tomatoes, peppers, mandarins, red onion, and thyme. Toss everything together.
  3. Before serving, pour the vinaigrette over the salad and toss again to coat all the ingredients. Enjoy!
Herb-infused Vinaigrette

Elevate the vinaigrette by infusing it with fresh herbs like basil, thyme, and mint. Blend the herbs with olive oil, vinegar, a touch of Dijon mustard, honey, and a pinch of salt and pepper. This herbaceous vinaigrette will take the flavors to a gourmet level and provide a delightful fragrance.

Grilled Vegetables

Instead of using raw vegetables, grill the tomatoes, red bell pepper, and red onion before adding them to the quinoa salad. Grilling will enhance their natural sweetness and add a smoky depth to the dish. Toss the grilled vegetables with the quinoa and mandarin oranges for a more sophisticated and savory twist.

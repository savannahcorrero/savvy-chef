Waffles + Cream

total time: 45 MINS | yield: 6-8 WAFFLES

DIFFICULTY

TAGS:
#BREAKFAST #SWEET #SUMMER

INGREDIENTS

  • 1⅔ cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 3⅔ cups milk, divided
  • 6 tbsp canola oil
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh raspberries

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In one bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, and salt. In another bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks. Add 1⅔ cups milk and oil to the egg yolks and stir this mixture into the dry ingredients until just moistened. Keep ¼ cup of the batter in a separate small bowl. In yet another bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gently fold them into the remaining batter.
  2. Cook the batter in a preheated waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions until the waffles turn golden brown.
  3. In a saucepan, heat sugar and the remaining milk until it becomes scalded. Take a small amount of this mixture and stir it into the reserved ¼ cup of batter. Then, return this combined mixture to the saucepan. Bring it to a boil and let it boil for 5-7 minutes or until it thickens. Remove the saucepan from heat, add vanilla, and mix well (the sauce will thicken further as it stands). Serve this sauce over the waffles and top it all with berries. Enjoy!
Whipped Cream + Chocolate

Top your waffles with a dollop of freshly whipped cream and some chocolate shavings or chocolate syrup. The combination of creamy whipped cream and indulgent chocolate will turn your waffles into a decadent dessert-like treat.

Caramelized Bananas + Pecans

Sauté banana slices in butter and brown sugar until they caramelize and become tender. Sprinkle toasted pecans on top for added crunch and nutty flavor. Layer this delicious combination over your waffles for a mouthwatering sweet and nutty twist.

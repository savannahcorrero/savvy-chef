French Crepes

prep time: 10 MINS | cook time: 10 MINS | additional time: 30 MINS

total time: 50 MINS | yield: 8 CREPES

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#BREAKFAST #SWEET #SAVORY

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1¼ cups whole milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Gently melt the unsalted butter in the microwave and allow it to cool slightly; if desired, speed up the cooling process by placing it in the refrigerator for a few minutes.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole milk, eggs, and melted butter, whisking them together until well incorporated.
  3. Add the all-purpose flour and salt to the mixture. Whisk until the batter achieves a smooth consistency, ensuring there are no lumps of flour remaining.
  4. Cover the bowl with a lid or plastic wrap and refrigerate it for approximately 30 minutes.
  5. Heat a 12" non-stick skillet or pan over medium heat. Lightly grease the pan with melted butter or nonstick cooking spray.
  6. Pour approximately ½ cup of the prepared batter into the center of the skillet. Swiftly tilt the pan in a circular motion to evenly spread the batter across the entire bottom surface.
  7. Allow the crepe to cook for approximately 1-2 minutes or until the edges begin to curl back and display a golden brown hue. With precision, flip the crepe to cook the other side until it also achieves a golden brown coloration.
  8. Repeat the cooking process with the remaining batter to yield a delightful stack of crepes. Add toppings as you please.
SAVORY
Spinach + Mushroom + Feta

Sauté fresh spinach and sliced mushrooms with a little olive oil and garlic until wilted and tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Spoon the mixture onto the center of a cooked crepe, sprinkle some crumbled feta cheese on top, and fold the crepe over the filling.

Chicken + Pesto

Cook seasoned and diced chicken until fully cooked and slightly browned. Spread a thin layer of pesto sauce on a cooked crepe, then add the cooked chicken pieces and some shredded mozzarella cheese. Fold the crepe and warm it slightly to melt the cheese.

Smoked Salmon + Cream Cheese

Spread a layer of cream cheese onto a cooked crepe. Add thin slices of smoked salmon, capers, and thinly sliced red onions. Roll the crepe and serve with a sprinkle of fresh dill on top.

SWEET
Nutella + Banana

Spread a generous layer of Nutella hazelnut spread on a cooked crepe. Add slices of ripe banana on top. Fold the crepe over the filling, and if desired, sprinkle some powdered sugar on top for an extra touch of sweetness.

Mixed Berry Compote

In a saucepan, combine a mix of fresh or frozen berries with a little sugar and a splash of water. Cook over medium heat until the berries break down and form a thick compote. Spoon the berry compote onto a cooked crepe and fold it over. Add a dollop of whipped cream on top!

Caramelized Apple + Cinnamon

Slice apples thinly and cook them in a pan with butter, brown sugar, and a pinch of cinnamon until they caramelize and become tender. Place the caramelized apple slices on a cooked crepe, drizzle some extra caramel sauce over them, and fold the crepe for a delightful treat.

Pumpkin Pancakes

prep time: 10 MINS | cook time: 10 MINS | additional time: 10 MINS

total time: 30 MINS | yield: 9 4½" pancakes

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#BREAKFAST #SWEET

INGREDIENTS

  • 1¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1½ tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup pumpkin purée
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, pumpkin pie spice, and salt until evenly mixed.
  2. In a separate bowl or measuring cup, whisk together the milk, pumpkin purée, melted butter, and egg.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, stirring just until combined.
  4. Heat a large pan over medium heat or preheat a griddle to 350°F. Lightly grease the pan with butter or vegetable oil.
  5. Spoon ¼ cup of batter into the pan at a time.
  6. Cook the pancakes on the first side until the edges start to look dry, about 2 to 3 minutes, then flip them over and continue cooking until the bottoms are browned, an additional 2 minutes.
  7. Serve immediately or transfer the cooked pancakes to the oven to stay warm if desired.
Cream Cheese Frosting

Add a dollop of cream cheese frosting on top of the pancakes. To make the frosting, mix softened cream cheese with powdered sugar and a splash of vanilla extract until smooth and creamy. Spread the frosting over the warm pancakes and drizzle with maple syrup for a delectable and indulgent treat.

Pecan Praline Topping

Create a delightful pecan praline topping to complement the pancakes. In a pan, melt butter with brown sugar until it forms a rich caramel sauce. Stir in chopped pecans and a pinch of salt. Pour the praline sauce over the pancakes for a crunchy and sweet nutty addition.

Cinnamon Whipped Cream

Prepare a luscious cinnamon whipped cream to serve with the pancakes. Whip chilled heavy cream with powdered sugar and ground cinnamon until soft peaks form. Serve a generous dollop of cinnamon whipped cream on top of the warm pancakes, adding a delightful creamy and spiced touch.

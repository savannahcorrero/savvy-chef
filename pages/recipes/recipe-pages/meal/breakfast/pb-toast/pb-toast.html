Peanut Butter Toast

total time: 5 MINS | yield: 2 SLICES

DIFFICULTY
TAGS:
#BREAKFAST #SWEET

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • ½ large banana, sliced
  • 2 tbsp granola
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Toast the 2 slices of whole grain bread to your desired level of toastiness.
  2. Spread half of the peanut butter on each slice of toast and arrange the sliced banana on top of the peanut butter.
  3. Sprinkle half of the brown sugar and ground cinnamon over the banana. Add the granola evenly over the banana slices.
  4. Finish with a sprinkle of pumpkin seeds. If desired, dust with a pinch of powdered sugar for an extra touch of sweetness.
Caramelized Bananas

Instead of simply adding sliced bananas, caramelize them for a rich and indulgent flavor. In a skillet, melt some butter and brown sugar over medium heat. Add the banana slices and cook until they become golden and caramelized. Top your peanut butter toast with these luscious caramelized bananas for a twist.

Lavender Honey + Toasted Almonds

Drizzle lavender-infused honey over the almond butter and banana, and top with a sprinkling of toasted almond slices. The floral notes of lavender honey and the crunch of toasted almonds will add an elegant spin to your toast.

Strawberry Balsamic Glaze Drizzle

Add sliced strawberries and a balsamic glaze. Make the glaze by gently heating balsamic vinegar until it thickens to a syrupy consistency. Let it cool slightly before drizzling it over the toast. The balsamic glaze will add a tangy sweetness that perfectly complements the peanut butter, banana, strawberries, and honey.
